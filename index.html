About Me

Prior to software development I was in the world of video editing and production. Over the years I’ve edited numerous television shows, music videos, television commercials, and corporate and non-profit films. The last 8 years of my career I started shooting and editing various projects for non-profits both in Tennessee and out of state. I have also created motion graphics designed DVD menu pages and built e-Learning courses, so UI/UX is of particular interest to me.

Web development has always intrigued me and I'm ready for a new challenge in my life.. I find that there are similar skills that are required in both video editing and developing. We are constantly trying to solve problems that arise and are under the demands of a timeline. I have spent years making clients happy and delivering their projects to them on time. This experience will be just as important in the world of web development.
